About Me

Hi, I’m Christopher “Topher-Two-Four” DeBonis.

I was born and raised in Albuquerque, NM.

I’m a game designer and developer, mainly using Unity and C#.

I paint ants, geese (not on them), and flying crayons.

I paint other things too.

I fucking love painting.

I’m an enjoyer of experimental and avant-garde media.

I am happily married to the woman of my dreams, and we have two amazing daughters together.

I have a studio that is in the process of being deployed, which I hope will make some kickass productions.

And canvases.

I’m probably the friendliest motherfucker you’ll meet.

If you’re interested in collaborating on a project, or would like to put in a commission request, please let me know!
