Ants and Geese!

I'm here to paint and make video games, and I'm all out of paint...

Hi, my name is Topher!

I'm an artist, born and raised in Albuquerque, NM.

I'm a game designer and developer, using primarily Unity and C#.

I'm a contemporary painter that speaks through figurative and organic abstraction.

I explore ideas by the methods and motif's set forth through experimental film and new media.

I engage in dialogue through artwork, to achieve a greater understanding of humanity.

I work to connect the analog, post-analog, and digital worlds.

I'm Topher :)
